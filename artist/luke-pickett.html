Luke Pickett

Luke Pickett was born in Melbourne, Australia. He attended RMIT's visual art program to study painting and drawing which destroyed the romantic illusions of making fine art as a career, and lead to an undying love of comics. He has been published in various anthologies both at home and aboard including Kagemono, Tango and Grawlix. His first comic book, Allocthonous Pop was released by Milkshadow Books in 2011. He lives in Toronto, Canada.
