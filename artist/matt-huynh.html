Matt Huynh

Creative Sydney Festival named Matt Huynh one of the most innovative contributors to Sydney's culture for his documentary graphic novel CAB, telling true stories from a suburban Sydney migrant community. Recently Matt launched his eleven meter comic strip documenting a Chinatown martial arts troupe at the Sydney Opera House for the 2011 Graphic Festival. He is currently working on a new graphic novel.
